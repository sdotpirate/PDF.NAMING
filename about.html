How to Use PIX to PDF

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Open the App: Launch PIX to PDF on your device.
  2. Select or Capture an Image: Choose an existing image from your gallery or take a new photo.
  3. Convert to PDF: Tap the convert button to transform your image into a PDF.
  4. Name Your File: Enter a custom name for your PDF. The file name is automatically formatted to include a timestamp, for example, "NewServiceRequest_00.00.00, 00:00AM".
  5. View or Share:
    • View PDF: Use the "View PDF" button to open the PDF in another tab, whether on mobile or desktop. The resulting file will follow the file naming structure (custom or default) no matter the platform.
    • Share or Download:
      • On mobile devices, you can share the PDF via email, messaging, cloud storage, and social media using the native share options on iOS and Android.
      • On desktop, the PDF is automatically downloaded to your computer for quick access and further sharing.
